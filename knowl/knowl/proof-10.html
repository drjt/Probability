Proof
\begin{align*} \sigma^2 = E[m(m-1)] + \mu - \mu^2 & = \SUM {m=0} {\infty} {m(m-1) \cdot \frac{\mu^m}{m!} \cdot e^{-\mu}} + \mu - \mu^2\\ & = e^{-\mu} \SUM {m=2} {\infty} {\frac{\mu^m}{(m-2)!} } + \mu - \mu^2\\ & = e^{-\mu} \cdot \mu^2 \SUM {m=2} {\infty} {\frac{\mu^{m-2}}{(m-2)!} } + \mu - \mu^2\\ & = e^{-\mu} \cdot \mu^2 \SUM {k=0} {\infty} {\frac{\mu^k}{k!} } + \mu - \mu^2\\ & = e^{-\mu} \cdot \mu^2 e^{\mu} + \mu - \mu^2\\ & = \mu^2 + \mu - \mu^2 \\ & = \mu \end{align*}
