Proof
\begin{align*} \mu = E[m] = & = \SUM {m=0} {\infty} {m \cdot \frac{(\lambda T)^m}{m!} \cdot e^{-\lambda T}}\\ & = e^{-\lambda T} \SUM {m=1} {\infty} {\frac{(\lambda T)^m}{(m-1)!} }\\ & = e^{-\lambda T} \cdot \lambda T \SUM {m=1} {\infty} {\frac{(\lambda T)^{m-1}}{(m-1)!} }\\ & = e^{-\lambda T} \cdot \lambda T \SUM {k=0} {\infty} {\frac{(\lambda T)^k}{k!} }\\ & = e^{-\lambda T} \cdot \lambda T e^{\lambda T}\\ & = \lambda T \end{align*}
