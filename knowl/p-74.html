Average Absolute Deviation from the Mean: \begin{equation*} \frac{\sum_{k=1}^n \left | x_k-\mu \right |}{n} \end{equation*} which, although nicely stated, is difficult to deal with algebraically since the absolute values do not simplify well algebraically. To avoid this algebraic roadblock, we can look for another way to nearly accomplish the same goal by squaring and then square rooting.
