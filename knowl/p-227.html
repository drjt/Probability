Consider \(f(x) = x^2/3\) over R = [-1,2]. Then, for \(-1 \le x \le 2\), \begin{equation*}F(x) = \int_{-1}^x u^2/3 du = x^3/9 + 1/9.\end{equation*} Notice, F(-1) = 0 since nothing has yet been accumulated over values smaller than -1 and F(2)=1 since by that time everything has been accumulated.
